okroshka

very delicious summer soup, refreshing

ingredients

  • potatoes
  • sausage
  • kefir
  • eggs chicken
  • redis
  • cucumber
  • greenery
  • water gas
  • sour cream

cooking method

  1. cook potatoes during 25 min
  2. slice sausage
  3. cook eggs during 10-12 min
  4. slice redis
  5. slice cucumber
  6. slice greenery
  7. mix all ingredients
  8. pour water gas, kefir and sour cream
  9. stir for 2 minutes
